    • Hey I'm Davis


    Welcome to my personal blog - a space where I document my journey as a developer, problem solver, and tech learner. I created this blog to share what I learn every day, break down complex concepts, and hopefully help someone who's walking the same path.


    What You'll Find Here


    Technical Articles

    I write simple, clear explanations of technical concepts — from programming fundamentals to advanced topics. If I learn something useful, I break it down here.

    Aptitude & Problem-Solving

    Concept notes, tricks, and strategies I use for aptitude tests and logical reasoning. Short, sharp, and practical.

    Interview Experiences

    My real interview experiences — what went right, what went wrong, the questions they asked, and how I prepared.

    LeetCode & DSA Solutions (My Approach)

    Not just answers — I share my thinking process, patterns, and different ways to approach a problem so you can develop your own style.

    Cybersecurity Stuff

    Notes, projects, and explanations related to security — from simple vulnerabilities to practical defenses.
